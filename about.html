The middle of the ocean is my favorite place in the world. There is something about it that captivates me. Whether it is the wind hitting my face, the sound of waves around me, or simply being far from land, I find a deep sense of calm near the water.

I am currently pursuing a Master of Science in Information Systems through the Kelley School of Business at Indiana University. My background in sailing, combined with my technical education, has helped me develop strong analytical and problem-solving skills. I’ve learned how to work with data, communicate clearly, and adapt quickly in changing environments.

Whether it’s reading wind patterns on the water or drawing insights from complex datasets, I bring a thoughtful and strategic mindset to everything I do. I hope to apply these skills in a future role as a business analyst or consultant, where I can help organizations solve problems, make data-driven decisions, and deliver meaningful results.

Feel free to reach me at nbalacha@iu.edu!
